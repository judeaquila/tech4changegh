Free, local coding lessons for basic and secondary school kids and teens

Beginning from local schools in the Volta Region of Ghana, Tech4Change GH is the place for free, fun and innovative coding lessons for kids and teens.

Our Impact Let's Collaborate

Volunteer at Tech4Change GH

As a volunteer, you will have the opportunity to choose from a range of fulfilling roles, including planning coding sessions, promoting workshops, communicating with parents of kids and teens, mentoring the young coders, curating learning resources, social media interactions, and many more.

Whether you have a few hours to spare or wish to be a regular mentor, your contribution will help us create a positive impact on the lives of aspiring young coders across Ghana. Together, we can unlock their potential and foster innovation within our community.

Become a Volunteer

Start a T4C Club

T4C Clubs are coding clubs in local schools and/or communities. You can take charge of setting up and maintaining a club.

All you need to do is to find a suitable venue to host the coding sessions and complete our simple application process. We will contact you and provide you with the resources, training and support you require. Plus, we have guidelines to help you manage your Club sessions.
