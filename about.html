What is Tech4Change GH

Who we are and What we do

About Us

At Tech4Change GH, we believe in shaping the future by equipping young minds with the transformative power of coding. As an initiative by Hologram Digital, a leading software and web development company, we are on a mission to provide free coding lessons to basic school and senior high school students in Ghana. In a technology-driven world, we understand the importance of fostering digital literacy from an early age, ensuring that every child has the opportunity to thrive in the digital era.

Our Mission

Tech4Change GH aims to bridge the digital divide by offering free coding lessons, enabling young learners to become creators of technology, rather than passive users.

Our Vision

To create a generation of tech-savvy African youth, empowered with coding skills to drive innovation and address challenges in the African sub-region.

Our Values

  • Inclusivity: We strive to provide equal opportunities for all students, regardless of their background or economic status.
  • Empowerment: We empower young minds with the knowledge and skills needed to unlock their full potential in the digital world.
  • Innovation: We foster a culture of innovation, encouraging creativity and problem-solving through coding.
  • Collaboration: We believe that collaboration with partners, volunteers, and the community is essential to drive meaningful change.
  • Impact: We measure success through the positive impact we create in the lives of our students and the African sub-region.

T4C Clubs

T4C Clubs are coding clubs in local schools and/or communities. Anyone can volunteer to set up and/or maintain a Club.

In schools, the ICT Teacher or any teacher passionate about impacting tech skills to kids and teens can set up a Club. Contact us and we will assist with the required resources.

In the community, all you need to do is to find a suitable venue to host the coding sessions and complete our simple application process. We will contact you and provide you with the resources, training and support you require. Plus, we have guidelines to help you manage your Club sessions.
